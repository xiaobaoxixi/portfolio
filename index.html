Hi,

My name is Yan Li and I am from Beijing, China. I moved to Europe over a decade ago and currently live in Copenhagen.


I've always beed drawn to technology and design, even though I didn't have the time to dig deeper in them. After years of academic training in other fields (Math and Law), and some unexpected turns in life, I finally decided to shift my focus and this year of being a Multimedia Design student has been wonderful!


As you might notice from my CV, I enjoy challenges and I am really good at learning new things. Knowledges aside, the whole experience of competitions, learning new languages every time I moved to a new country and use it for study and developing different ways of thinking and analysing based on different academic disciplines are all very helpful for me to understand my abilities and limits, which in turn helps me to face new challeneges. This ascending spiral will for sure continue in my approach to become a Multimedia Designer / Web development.
