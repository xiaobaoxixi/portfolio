Hi,

My name is Yan Li and I am from Beijing, China. I moved to Europe over a decade ago and currently live in Copenhagen.


I've always beed drawn to technology and design. After years of academic training in other fields (Math and Law), and some unexpected turns in life, I finally decided to shift my focus and this year of learning multimedia design and front end development has been wonderful!


As you might notice from my CV, I enjoy challenges and I am really good at learning new things. This ascending spiral will for sure continue in my approach to become a web developer / multimedia designer.
